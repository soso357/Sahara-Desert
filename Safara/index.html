Sahara

The Sahara Desert is the largest hot desert in the world, covering most of Northern Africa.The Sahara spans an area of approximately 9.2 million square kilometers (3.6 million square miles), making it comparable in size to the entire United States or China. It stretches across several countries, including Algeria, Chad, Egypt, Libya, Mali, Mauritania, Morocco, Niger, Sudan, and Tunisia.
